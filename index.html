❓ FAQ
Who's invited?
All Purple people, your registered +1 partner, and your kids. We've got activities for the little ones too.
What's included with the wristband?
Unlimited access to the swimming pond, restrooms, and showers throughout the event. Plus everything food and drink related, including the ice cream stand.
Can I bring my dog?
Unfortunately no. The venue is a public swimming area and dogs aren't permitted on site. Sorry, and thanks for understanding.
Why Biotop Komárov?
It ticks all the boxes: pool to kick off summer, a large fenced area just for us, a campfire spot for špekáčky, and party mode on without disturbing anyone.
Until when does it go?
From 15:00 until late evening. Or till 3:00 AM for the truly dedicated ones.
